# Limit or Better A training app for people who lift, run, or both. Sets, reps, runs, recovery — all on your device. --- ## Support If you have a question, bug report, or feature suggestion, get in touch: - **GitHub Issues:** [github.com/limitorbetter/limit-or-better/issues](https://github.com/limitorbetter/limit-or-better/issues) I'm a solo developer and I read every message, but replies may take a few days. --- ## Privacy Limit or Better stores all your training data on your device. The app does not collect, transmit, or sync any personal data. There is no account, no cloud, and no analytics. If you enable GPS for run tracking, your location is used only during the run to calculate distance and pace. It is stored on your device and never transmitted anywhere. You can read the full privacy policy on the Me page of the app. --- ## Frequently asked questions **Where is my data stored?** On your device only. If you delete the app, your data is deleted with it. Back up your phone to keep a copy. **Can I sync between devices?** Not currently. The app has no cloud component by design. **Will there be an Android version?** Yes, I plan to make that next **How do I export my data?** Yu can export to CSV from the Me page --- *Limit or Better is developed and maintained by Neil Christie © 2026.*
